JAIN UNIVERSITY FET HOSTEL FACILITY

HOSTEL OVERVIEW
The hostel facility at Jain (Deemed-to-be University) Faculty of Engineering and Technology is designed as a home away from home for students.
Residential arrangements are separate for boys and girls.
The Hostel Management Division manages university accommodations.
The facilities are focused on student comfort and quality of life.

CAUTION DEPOSIT
Refundable Caution Deposit: INR 10,000
The caution deposit is returned when the student exits the hostel at the end of the tenure, subject to room condition and dues.

IMPORTANT FEE AND DAMAGE NOTES
- Students must hand over their rooms in the same condition in which they were received.
- Breakages, damages, or missing items may reduce or affect the refundable caution amount.
- In case of damage, the student must pay the damage charges and collect the NOC from the hostel.

HOSTEL RULES AND REGULATIONS
- Students must strictly follow current and future hostel rules.
- Hostel accommodation is a privilege, not a right.
- The university may refuse accommodation for gross rule violations or potential disturbances.
- Disciplinary action, including permanent expulsion, may be taken for violating hostel rules.
- Students are expected to remain hostel inmates throughout the year unless debarred on disciplinary grounds.
- Room rent for both terms may apply regardless of continuous occupancy.
- Students must know and follow all hostel rules; ignorance is not accepted as an excuse.
- Students must maintain room furniture and electrical fittings in good condition.
- Students may be required to vacate the hostel during summer vacation.
- Students are responsible for damage to hostel property.
- If the responsible person for damage cannot be identified, the cost may be shared equally among residents concerned.
- Disciplinary measures are intended to maintain peace and order in the hostel.
