ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
The Cloud Club is a student-led community focused on cloud computing, DevOps, and modern technologies. It provides students with opportunities to learn and innovate through hands-on workshops, events, and collaborative projects in platforms like AWS, Azure, and GCP. The club aims to build technical expertise, industry readiness, and a strong community of cloud enthusiasts.
