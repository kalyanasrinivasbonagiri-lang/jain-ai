ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
Zigbee is a student-led technical club under the CSE - Internet of Things (IoT) branch at Jain University. It focuses on innovation, research, and hands-on learning in emerging technologies for IoT students. The club actively organizes events like hackathons, workshops, and research initiatives such as Rezonix Researchthon, Talent Surge, and Raspberry Pi workshops to help students build practical skills in IoT, web development, and modern tech domains. It provides a collaborative platform for students to explore, create, and grow in the field of technology.
