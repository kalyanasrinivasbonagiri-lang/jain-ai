JAIN UNIVERSITY CLUBS OVERVIEW

Jain University has multiple student clubs across technology, culture, research, and innovation. These clubs help students explore their interests, build practical skills, participate in events, and connect with like-minded peers.

ALL CLUBS WITH BRANCH OR SCHOOL
1. ENIGMA - Branch / School: CSE and CSE STAR
2. Zigbee - Branch / School: CSE - Internet of Things (IoT)
3. Cognito Club - Branch / School: CTIS
4. Cultural Club FET_JU - Branch / School: Culturals
5. Neuron.AI Club - Branch / School: CSE (AI)
6. The Cloud Club - Branch / School: Not Mentioned
7. GRI (Center of Grassroots Research and Innovation) - Branch / School: Research and Innovation

CLUB DETAILS
1. ENIGMA
Branch / School: CSE and CSE STAR
Description: ENIGMA is the technical club for the CSE and CSE STAR branches. It focuses on innovation, technical excellence, and creative problem-solving.

2. Zigbee
Branch / School: CSE - Internet of Things (IoT)
Description: Zigbee is the club for IoT students and focuses on emerging technologies, hands-on workshops, research, and technical skill-building.

3. Cognito Club
Branch / School: CTIS
Description: Cognito Club is the CTIS club focused on Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, innovation, workshops, and hackathons.

4. Cultural Club FET_JU
Branch / School: Culturals
Description: The Cultural Club FET_JU provides a platform for students interested in dance, music, photography, visual arts, and other cultural activities.

5. Neuron.AI Club
Branch / School: CSE (AI)
Description: Neuron.AI Club is the club for CSE (AI) students and focuses on artificial intelligence, modern technologies, workshops, and technical events.

6. The Cloud Club
Branch / School: Not Mentioned
Description: The Cloud Club focuses on cloud computing, DevOps, and modern platforms such as AWS, Azure, and GCP.

7. GRI (Center of Grassroots Research and Innovation)
Branch / School: Research and Innovation
Description: GRI focuses on rural impact, grassroots innovation, sustainability, field visits, and research-driven problem-solving.

SUMMARY
Jain University currently has clubs related to CSE, CSE AI, IoT, CTIS, culture, cloud computing, and research and innovation.
