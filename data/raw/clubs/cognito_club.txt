ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
The Cognito Club is a student-led initiative for the CTIS branch at JAIN (Deemed-to-be University), focused on innovation in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. It provides a collaborative environment where CTIS students can learn, build, and grow through workshops, challenges, hackathons, and real-world problem-solving. The club aims to empower students with technical skills, industry exposure, and a strong community to drive future innovation.
