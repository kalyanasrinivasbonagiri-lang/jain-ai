ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
GRI (Center of Grassroots Research and Innovation) is an initiative at Jain University focused on empowering students to create sustainable solutions for rural communities. The center promotes experiential learning through village visits, field research, and collaboration with local communities, combining ancestral wisdom with modern technology to solve real-world challenges.
