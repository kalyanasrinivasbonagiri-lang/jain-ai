ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
Neuron.AI Club is a student-driven initiative under the CSE (AI) branch at Jain University. It focuses on building knowledge and skills in artificial intelligence and modern technologies for CSE (AI) students. The club organizes events, workshops, and technical sessions like Tech Xplore and full-stack web development workshops to help students explore, learn, and innovate. It provides a collaborative platform for aspiring developers and AI enthusiasts to enhance their practical skills and stay updated with emerging tech trends.
