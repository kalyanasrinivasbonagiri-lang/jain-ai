ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
Infosphere is a technical student club under the Department of Information Science and Engineering at Jain (Deemed-to-be University). It empowers students through technical excellence, innovation, and entrepreneurial thinking by providing a collaborative platform for workshops, coding challenges, and real-world projects. The club also promotes leadership, teamwork, and interdisciplinary learning to help students build impactful technological solutions.
