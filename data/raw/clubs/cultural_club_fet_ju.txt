ABOUT THE CLUB
Short Description:
The Cultural Club FET_JU is a vibrant student community that celebrates creativity and talent across various art forms, including dance, music, photography, and visual arts. It provides a platform for students to express their passion, showcase their skills, and participate in engaging cultural events like Infinity 2k25, Ink-Finity War, Youth Parliament, and Kannada Rajyotsava, fostering creativity and cultural spirit on campus.
