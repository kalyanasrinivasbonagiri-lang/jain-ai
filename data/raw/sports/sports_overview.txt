Title: Sports at Jain University FET

Overview:
Sports play an important role at the Faculty of Engineering and Technology (FET), Jain University. The university encourages students to balance academics with athletics and promotes teamwork, discipline, fitness, and leadership through active participation in sports. FET supports a strong sports culture through training, competitions, and major campus events. Students participate in a variety of sports such as football, cricket, basketball, volleyball, throwball, frisbee, athletics, badminton, and chess. Major events like Sportastica 2025 and MOJO Pravah 2.0 2026 reflect the university's commitment to building a vibrant and competitive sports environment.

Sports Culture at FET:
The sports culture at Jain University FET is built around participation, competition, and team spirit. Students are encouraged to take part in both internal and external tournaments, helping them develop confidence, discipline, and collaboration. Sports are considered an important part of overall student development and campus life.

Major Sports at Jain University:
- Football
- Cricket
- Basketball
- Volleyball
- Throwball
- Frisbee
- Athletics
- Badminton
- Chess

Event: Sportastica 2025
Sportastica 2025 is an inter-departmental sports fest organized by Jain University FET. It promotes healthy competition, sportsmanship, and unity among students from different departments.

Sports included in Sportastica 2025:
- Basketball
- Football
- Cricket
- Volleyball
- Throwball
- Athletics

Event: MOJO Pravah 2.0 2026
MOJO Pravah 2.0 2026 is a major event organized by Jain University FET in association with MOJO Campus. It combines sports and cultural activities and provides students with a broader platform for participation and engagement.

Sports included in MOJO Pravah 2.0 2026:
- Cricket
- Football
- Badminton
- Chess
- Volleyball

Summary:
Sports are an essential part of student life at Jain University FET. Through events like Sportastica 2025 and MOJO Pravah 2.0 2026, the university creates opportunities for students to build athletic skills, teamwork, and a strong campus spirit.
