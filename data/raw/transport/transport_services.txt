JAIN UNIVERSITY FET TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

TRANSPORT OVERVIEW
Jain (Deemed-to-be University) Faculty of Engineering and Technology provides transportation services for faculty and students from different parts of the city to the campus.
The buses are described as comfortable and safe.
The transport service is subsidized by the university.

TRANSPORT FEE
