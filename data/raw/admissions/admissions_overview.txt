JAIN UNIVERSITY B.TECH ADMISSIONS OVERVIEW

This file contains admission information for B.Tech programs at Jain University based on the provided 2026 admission details.

PROGRAM LEVEL
Program Type: B.Tech (Regular)
Course Duration: 4 years
Semesters: 8
Mode of Application: Online only

ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY
Minimum Qualification: Passed 12th PUC / ISC / CBSE or equivalent board
Minimum Marks: 45% marks
Minimum Marks for SC/ST: 40% marks
Compulsory Subjects: Physics and Mathematics
Accepted Third Subject: Chemistry / Electronics / Computer Science / Biology
IB Eligibility: Minimum 26 credits
Cambridge Eligibility: 3 subjects at A Level including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
Final Year Applicants: Candidates awaiting Class XII or equivalent results may also apply
NIOS Eligibility: NIOS candidates are eligible to apply

ENTRANCE SCORES ACCEPTED
- UNIGAUGE
- JEE
- ComedK

APPLICATION PROCESS/how to apply for jain /where to apply for jain university
Step 1: New User Registration
- Enter all the relevant details carefully.
- An email will be sent by Jain University to authenticate the email ID.
- Authentication is required to continue the admission application process.
- All communication will be sent to the registered email ID and mobile number.
- Requests to change mobile number, email ID, or correspondence address will not be entertained.

Step 2: Application Payment
- Payment Mode: Online through Net banking, Credit card, or Debit card
- Application Cost: Rs. 600 inclusive of GST for general candidates

Step 3: Fill Online Application Form
- Enter personal information
- Enter address information
- Enter academic information
- Enter additional information
- Upload recent passport size colour photograph
- Upload Class X mark sheet
- After submission, an application number is generated
- Use the application number for future correspondence

UPLOAD REQUIREMENTS
Photograph Requirement: 3.5 x 4.5 cm colour photograph
Photograph Format: .jpg or .png
Photograph Maximum Size: 50 KB
Class X Mark Sheet Formats: .jpg, .png, or .pdf
Class X Mark Sheet Maximum Size: 100 KB
